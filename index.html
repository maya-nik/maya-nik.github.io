Keeper of The Lost Cities

About

This is a series of 11 books, each one of them is more interesting than the last. It is about a twelve year old girl with secret powers, who then discovers that she is an elf and has to save herself and her friends from the “bad guys“ who call themselves the “neverseen“. Sophie finds a lot of new friends and even more secrets about her past.
