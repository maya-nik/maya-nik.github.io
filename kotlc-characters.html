Characters

  1. Sophie Foster(blonde girl in the middle)
    Sophie Foster is the main character of this book series. She is usually shy and very oblivious, but when one of the people that she loves get hurt or almost dies, she freaks out and goes on an anger rampage to try and stop the enemies that have tried to hurt and kidnap her(and her friends) multiple times. Sophies abilities are:
    • a telepath(she can read peoples minds)
    • an inflictor(she can make people feel different emotions)
    • a polyglot(she can speak and understand all languages)
    • a teleporter(she can teleport from one place to another while free falling)
    • an enhancer(she can make other peoples abilities stronger)
  2. Keefe Sencen(blonde guy in the middle)
    Keefe Sencen is a very powerful empath, he is Sophie's most handsome friend. He cares about Sophie more than he cares about anyone else. He grew up in a horrible household where both his parents were awful to him. Keefe always tries to do whats best for Sophie even though it also sometimes hurts both him and her.
  3. Dex Diznee(guy all the way to the left)
    Dex Diznee is an amazing and very underrated guy. His parents are a bad match and because of that he and his younger siblings(who are triplets have been looked down upon their whole life).He is a tecnopath and Sophies best friend. Dex has supported Sophie ever since they met and he always wants to help her achieve lots of things. Dex was Sophies first kiss and Sophie was his.
  4. Biana Vacker(brown haired girl with braid)
    Biana Vacker is the youngest sibling of the prestigious Vacker Family. She is a Vanisher and she really likes Sparkles. She has always been looked down on, because she was the youngest and everyone considered her to be the pretty pink princess even though she has almost died saving her friends multiple times and she has scars to prove it.
  5. Fitz Vacker(brown haired guy next to Biana)
    Fitz Vacker is the person who brought Sophie to the Lost Cities and who saved her many times. Even though he has always helped Sophie he has hurt her just as much. Fitz has anger issues and whenever something about his brother comes up he gets angry and most of the time he lets his rage out on Sophie which hurts her a lot. He apologizes later and Sophie always forgiives him even though he doesn't deserve it. He is a Telepath just like Sophie.
  6. Tam Song(black haired guy with silver bangs)
    Tam Song is just like Keefe his parents kicked him and his sister out of the house, because of their abilities. Tam trusts very little and he would do anything to protect his sister. Tam is a Shade so he can manipulate shadows.
  7. Linh Song(black haired girl on the right)
    Linh Song is a Hydrokinetic she can manipulate water
    and form it into any shape she wants.Linh is a very
    sweet girl and she loves animals.
  8. Wylie Endal(guy with cropped hair all the way on the right)
    Wylie Endal is a Flasher just like his mom, they can manipulate light. Wylies father was exiled when he was very young and his mother died not long after. He grew up with his fathers best friend. At first Wylie didn't like Sophie, because of her involvement with his dad being exiled, but soon he forgave her and helped her fight the Neverseen.
